About ISTE


Indian society for technical education (ISTE) is a national level nonprofitable society which was started in the year 1941 as Association of Principals of Technical Instistutions (APTI). In 1968, it was converted to ISTE.

The Primary goal of the ISTE is to offer high-quality training programs for teachers in technical institutions enabling them to enhance thier knowledge and skills within thier respective areas. This, inturn helps them play a crucial role in nurturing and contributing to the growth of skilled professional engineers that are in demand by variousÂ industries.

Membership Benefits


  • ISTE New Delhi will recognize you as a member of a national professional society and will issue membership cards accordingly.

  • You'll be part of a large academic community with over 1,24,000 professionals, receiving priority access to short-term training for academic improvement and career advancement opportunities.

  • You can engage in academic events at the chapter, section and national levels, as well as join activities hosted by prestigious institutions like IITs and NITs.

  • You can make use of exclusive discounts on the participation fee during the annual fest "NIPUNA."
